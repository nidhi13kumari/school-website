Welcome To EDU-SHIELD INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL, Patna

Martin Luther King Jr. said, 'The function of education is to teach one to think intensively and to think critically. Intelligence plus character - that is the goal of true education.'

Keeping with the same ideology, we at Delhi Public School, Gaya, endeavour to impart education, a combination of Indian values and the Western concept of a pragmatic approach to knowledge acquisition. The National Olympiads are a regular feature of the school. Delhi Public School, Gaya, is a place which prides itself in being a unique model of all-inclusive learning that best suits students who are passionately driven, curious and self-sufficient.

Delhi Public School Gaya is affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), a giant educational board in the country.
